About Apex Plumbing Projects

PROUDLY INDIGENOUS-OWNED · SUPPLY NATION CERTIFIED

Professional. Reliable. Experienced.

Apex Plumbing Projects is a full-service plumbing company providing dependable residential, commercial and industrial plumbing solutions across the Central Coast, Newcastle, Lake Macquarie and the Hunter Valley.

Founded by Phillip James, Apex has grown to a team of 12 experienced plumbers committed to quality workmanship, clear communication, and getting the job done right the first time. We are licensed master plumbers, fully insured, and proud to be Supply Nation certified.

At the heart of our operations is a strong safety culture. We place the utmost importance on the well-being of both our team and our clients.
