Blocked Drains

CCTV inspection and high-pressure jetting from $99.

$0 call out Upfront pricing Lifetime warranty Same-day service

Blocked drains are one of the most common and most frustrating plumbing problems. At Apex Plumbing Projects, we use CCTV drain camera technology and high-pressure water jetting equipment to quickly diagnose and clear any blockage, from simple kitchen sink clogs to major sewer line obstructions.

Every blocked drain job starts with a CCTV inspection to identify the exact cause and location. This means no guesswork, no unnecessary digging, and no recurring problems. Common causes include tree root intrusion, fat and grease buildup, foreign objects, collapsed pipes, and sediment accumulation.
